Frequently Asked Questions

Admission requirements include completed application, previous academic records, entrance assessment, and an interview with parents.

Yes, we offer merit-based and need-based scholarships. Contact our admissions office for more information.

What are the school fees?

School fees vary by grade level. Please contact our finance office for a detailed breakdown.
